AI asset offeringc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo#AIAssetOffering

A catalog-record entry that registers an AI model in a dataspace catalog, bundling the model reference, the providing agent, and an ODRL offer policy. Specialises dcat:CatalogRecord; carries an odrl:Offer via daimo:hasOfferPolicy. In DSP terms this is the catalog-time artefact that becomes a dspace:ContractOffer during negotiation.
A catalog-record entry registering an AI model in a dataspace catalog as a governed offer, bundling the model, the issuing agent, and an ODRL offer policy.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo
Example 
A research organisation publishing one of its machine-learning models into a dataspace catalog declares an AIAssetOffering that references the model (via offersModel), the organisation itself (via offeredBy), and an ODRL offer policy (via hasOfferPolicy) permitting use and distribution under a specific licence.

execution authorizationc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo#ExecutionAuthorization

An ODRL agreement (produced by a DSP contract negotiation) that binds at least one odrl:permission to concrete it6:Run invocations it authorises. Specialises odrl:Agreement; MUST carry at least one odrl:permission per the ODRL policy model.
An ODRL agreement, produced by a Dataspace-Protocol contract negotiation, that binds specific ODRL permissions to concrete MLDCAT-AP run invocations it authorises, with an expiry timestamp and a grantee agent.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo
Example 
After a successful DSP contract negotiation, the resulting agreement is represented as an ExecutionAuthorization granting odrl:use to the consumer (daimo:grantedTo), covering one or more runs via daimo:authorizesRun, expiring at a specified daimo:expiresAt timestamp.

Agreement c

is in range of

A dataspace-scoped role taken by a participant with respect to AI assets. Anti-rigid: an agent can gain and lose roles over time. Subclasses are intentionally NOT mutually disjoint — one agent can hold several roles simultaneously (e.g., a research group may be both Evaluator and Model Provider).
A dataspace-scoped role played by a participant with respect to AI assets (anti-rigid; an agent gains and loses roles over time).

https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo
Example 
An agent acting as a Model Provider within a given EDC participant context has a ParticipantRole instance of type ModelProvider connected via daimo:hasRole and scoped by daimo:inParticipantContext.

shared evaluation contextc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo#SharedEvaluationContext

A reified grouping of the conditions under which model results are comparable: task, dataset, dataset version, protocol, and random seed. Does not claim to cover every facet that affects comparability (metric, split strategy, hyperparameters); it is the minimum grouping required by CQ-V1..CQ-V3.
A reified grouping of the conditions under which model evaluations are comparable: task, dataset, dataset version, evaluation protocol, and random seed.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo
Example 
Two candidate models evaluated on the same SharedEvaluationContext (identical it6:Task, dcat:Dataset, daimo:datasetVersion, daimo:protocol, and daimo:randomSeed) yield metric values that are directly comparable; changing any of the five facets breaks comparability.

offered byop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/pionera/daimo#offeredBy

Links an offering to the agent that authored the offered AI model (i.e., the model provider). Functional: each offering has exactly one offering agent. Deliberately NOT declared rdfs:subPropertyOf dct:publisher: on a dcat:CatalogRecord, dct:publisher denotes the catalog maintainer (the platform registering the record), not the author of the asset being registered. Conflating them would entail that the catalog-time publisher is the model creator — semantically wrong in a real dataspace where e.g. INESData publishes records authored by UPM.

has characteristics: functional
